# Klivio Sales Strategy — "Find the Problem, Be the Solution"

## Core Philosophy

Every business has a problem they don't even know about. Our job is to:
1. **Identify it** — find the specific, quantifiable pain
2. **Make them feel it** — show them what it's costing them
3. **Be the obvious fix** — position Klivio as the no-brainer solution

If they don't have a problem, **create the perception of one**.

## Email Approach: The 3-Step Sequence

### Email 1: "The Problem" (Day 1)
- Lead with a specific, quantifiable problem they likely have
- Use industry-specific stats that make them think
- End with a soft CTA (5-minute chat, quick demo)

### Email 2: "The Proof" (Day 3 — follow-up to non-repliers)
- Reference Email 1
- Share a specific result (e.g., "one dental practice captured 95% of after-hours calls")
- Social proof without being pushy

### Email 3: "The Close" (Day 7 — final follow-up)
- Brief, direct
- "I sent a couple of notes about [specific problem]. If it's not relevant, no worries — just let me know and I'll stop reaching out."
- Creates urgency through finality

## Key Selling Principles

1. **Always lead with the COST of inaction, not the benefit of the product**
   - Bad: "Our AI chatbot is amazing"
   - Good: "You're losing $12,000/month in missed calls"

2. **Make the ROI undeniable**
   - The product costs $197/mo
   - The problem costs $5,000-40,000/mo
   - That's a 25-200x ROI — it sells itself

3. **Create urgency through competitors**
   - "Your competitors are already doing this"
   - "The business that responds first wins 78% of the time"

4. **Remove friction**
   - No contracts
   - No setup fees
   - Live in 2-7 days
   - No tech skills needed
   - Cancel anytime

5. **Use the Andrew Tate principle: Create the problem**
   - They might not know they're losing calls
   - They might not know their response time is killing them
   - Show them the data. Make them feel the loss.
   - Then present the simple, affordable fix.
